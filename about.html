About Local Craft

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Local Craft began as a small workshop with a big vision: to preserve traditional woodworking craftsmanship while embracing modern sustainability practices. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a team of skilled craftsmen dedicated to creating exceptional pieces for both residential and commercial clients.

Our Mission

To become the most trusted carpentry and building solution in South Africa that preserves traditional craftsmanship while embracing modern sustainability.

Our Values

  • Quality craftsmanship in every project
  • Sustainable sourcing of materials
  • Attention to detail and precision
  • Customer satisfaction as our top priority
  • Innovation in design and technique
